History of Federal College of Education,Katsina

Federal College of Education Katsina,was founded in 1976, by the Federal Government of Nigeria along with other Federal Colleges of Education (then Federal Advanced Teacher's College) to produce well qualified non-graduate teachers of the Nigeria Certificate in Education cadre initially to teach in the Junior Secondary Schools in the Country.
